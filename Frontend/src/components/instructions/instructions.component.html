Customer Instructions

1. How to pay a bill?

  1. Click on View Bills.
  2. If you see bills with status Unpaid, click their checkbox to select those bills.
  3. Click on the Proceed to Pay button.
  4. You will be redirected to the bill summary (you can remove a bill if you selected it by mistake).
  5. Choose your preferred Payment Method.
  6. Complete the payment and download your invoice.

2. Disconnected? How to raise a complaint?

  1. Go to the Register Complaint page.
  2. Enter your category, or if not listed, select Others.
  3. Select your preferred mode of contact.
  4. Provide a description if any.
  5. Submit.
  6. To check complaint status, click the Complaint Status button in the navigation bar.

3. How to edit your customer profile?

  1. Click on the Profile option from the navigation bar.
  2. Your existing profile details will be displayed.
  3. Click the Edit Profile button.
  4. Update the fields you want to modify (Name, Email, Mobile, Address, etc.).
  5. After making changes, click on the Save or Update button.
  6. A confirmation message will appear if the update is successful.
  7. You can revisit the Profile page anytime to verify your updated details.
